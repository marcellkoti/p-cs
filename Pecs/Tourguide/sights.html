Sights

  1. The Early Christian Necropolis of Pécs, extensive Roman tombs and mausoleums dating from around the 4th century, and a UNESCO World Heritage Site).
  2. Cella Septichora (4th century)
  3. The Cathedral (11th century, renovated in the 19th century).
  4. Hungarian Bishop's Palace (12th century)
  5. University of Pécs (1367), building of the Faculty of Science and Faculty of Humanities in Ifjúság street. It includes a Botanical Garden.
  6. Klimo Library (1774). The first public library in Hungary
